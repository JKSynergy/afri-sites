About Afrisites Tours & Travel

Creating memorable travel experiences across Uganda, East Africa, and beyond

We deliver seamless, memorable, and personalized travel experiences.

Afrisites Tours and Travel (U) Limited is a fully registered Ugandan company offering exceptional tourism experiences across Uganda and East Africa. Through strong partnerships with top destinations, we provide relaxation, luxury, adventure, and fun — always aiming to exceed our clients' expectations.
